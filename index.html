Questions?

Commonly Asked Questions

Learn more about how BarakahFlow handles your financial data and religious obligations.

Is BarakahFlow truly designed with Islamic finance principles in mind?

Yes. BarakahFlow is built to respect Islamic financial principles. We avoid interest-based calculations and provide specific categories for Zakat, Sadaqah, and purification of wealth. Our focus is on fostering "Barakah" through ethical money management.

How does the Zakat calculator work?

The app pulls real-time silver market data to determine the current Niṣāb. By aggregating your assets (cash, silver, business inventory), it calculates exactly what you owe when your wealth has stayed above the threshold for a lunar year (Hawl).

Is my financial data secure and private?

We believe financial privacy is a fundamental right. BarakahFlow operates without ads or third-party trackers. Your financial data is stored locally on your device and is never sold or uploaded to external servers without your explicit action.
